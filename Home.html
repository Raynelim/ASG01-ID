About the Anime

Windbreaker is an anime that follows the thrilling story of street racing, friendship, and determination. Dive into intense races, compelling characters, and the journey of becoming the fastest. This fan site is dedicated to providing info, characters, and episodes for all Windbreaker fans!

The main protagonist, Haruka Sakura, is a delinquent who only cares about fighting to reach the top. He moves to the town of Furin to attend Furin High School, famous for its powerful fighters. However, Furin High School's students are now known as the "Bofurin," protectors of the town, fighting to maintain peace and order. Sakura must learn the value of teamwork and fighting for others if he wants to be accepted into their ranks and truly belong.
