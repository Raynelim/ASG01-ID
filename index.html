The End Of The World

Across the ever-changing lands of Zephyraen, mighty mechanical beasts battle for power and survival. Some fight with honor, protecting the territories they have guarded for generations. Others embrace chaos, expanding their influence and threatening the balance of the world. As you enter this realm, every creature you meet has its own past and purpose. Your choices shape their paths, influence battles, and determine which factions rise or fall. The fate of Zephyraen — whether it thrives, collapses, or transforms — rests in your hands.
