Character Overview

Kyutaro Sugishita is a second-year student at Furin High School and one of the most formidable fighters in Bofurin. Distinguished by his tall stature, long dark hair, and perpetually serious expression, Sugishita is known for his near-silent demeanor and unwavering devotion to Hajime Umemiya. He rarely speaks, preferring to let his actions and incredible fighting prowess communicate for him.

Despite his intimidating appearance and violent fighting style, Sugishita harbors deep respect and loyalty toward Umemiya, whom he considers the ultimate authority. His dedication borders on fanatical—he becomes instantly hostile toward anyone he perceives as disrespecting or threatening Umemiya. This fierce protectiveness makes him both a powerful ally and a dangerous adversary, as he will stop at nothing to defend those he deems worthy of his loyalty.

Personality & Traits

Silent & Stoic

Sugishita rarely speaks, communicating primarily through glares, grunts, and physical actions. His silence adds to his intimidating presence, and when he does speak, his words carry significant weight. He sees no need for unnecessary conversation, believing that strength and actions are more meaningful than words.

Absolute Loyalty

His devotion to Umemiya is absolute and unwavering. Sugishita views Umemiya as the person most worthy of his respect and will follow his orders without question. Anyone who shows disrespect to Umemiya becomes an immediate enemy in Sugishita's eyes, triggering his fierce protective instincts and violent response.

Fierce Combat Style

In battle, Sugishita is brutal and unrelenting, showing no mercy to opponents. His fighting style is straightforward but devastatingly effective, relying on overwhelming physical power and aggression. He fights with complete commitment, viewing combat as the truest form of communication and respect between warriors.

Background Story

Meeting Umemiya

Sugishita's life changed when he encountered Hajime Umemiya. While the exact circumstances remain largely unspoken, it's clear that Umemiya earned Sugishita's complete respect and loyalty through his strength, character, and leadership. From that moment, Sugishita dedicated himself entirely to following Umemiya and protecting what he values.

Rise in Bofurin

Despite being only a second-year, Sugishita's exceptional combat abilities and unwavering loyalty to Umemiya have earned him a position of significant respect within Bofurin. His strength and dedication make him one of the organization's most formidable fighters, capable of handling threats that would overwhelm multiple ordinary members.

Rivalry with Sakura

Initially hostile toward Haruka Sakura due to perceiving disrespect toward Umemiya, Sugishita viewed the first-year as an enemy. However, as Sakura proved his worth and earned Umemiya's acknowledgment, their relationship evolved into something resembling grudging respect, though still marked by competitive tension and occasional clashes.

Current Role

Sugishita serves as one of Umemiya's most trusted fighters and protectors. While he holds no official title, his reputation and strength make him a de facto enforcer within Bofurin. He continues to support Umemiya's vision through action rather than words, demonstrating his loyalty through unwavering dedication and devastating combat performance when needed.
