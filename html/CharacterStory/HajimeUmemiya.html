Character Overview

Hajime Umemiya is the charismatic leader of Bofurin and a third-year student at Furin High School. Known for his bright personality, infectious smile, and genuine care for others, Umemiya has transformed Furin from a school of chaos into a force for protecting the community. His leadership style emphasizes bonds, family, and fighting for what truly matters rather than for dominance or ego.

Despite his warm and approachable demeanor, Umemiya is an exceptionally powerful fighter who commands absolute respect from both allies and enemies. His strength lies not just in his combat abilities but in his ability to inspire loyalty, unite people, and bring out the best in those around him. He maintains a rooftop garden at the school, symbolizing his nurturing nature and belief in growth and cultivation—both of plants and people.

Personality & Traits

Charismatic Leadership

Umemiya possesses natural leadership abilities that inspire unwavering loyalty from his followers. He leads by example, never asking others to do what he wouldn't do himself. His genuine care for each member of Bofurin creates a family atmosphere where everyone feels valued and motivated to protect their community and each other.

Warm & Nurturing

Beneath his powerful fighter exterior, Umemiya has a deeply caring and gentle nature. He tends his rooftop garden with the same devotion he shows his team members, believing in patience, nurturing, and helping things grow. He remembers small details about people and makes everyone feel important and seen.

Protective Instinct

Umemiya's core motivation is protecting those he considers family and the town they call home. When those under his protection are threatened, his friendly demeanor can shift to reveal a fierce and intimidating side. His strength is wielded not for personal glory but to shield others from harm and maintain peace in the community.

Background Story

Difficult Past

Umemiya's early life was marked by hardship and loss, experiences that shaped his compassionate leadership style. These struggles taught him the value of having people to rely on and the importance of protecting those bonds. His past fuels his determination to create a better environment for others at Furin High.

Transforming Furin

When Umemiya rose to leadership at Furin High, he fundamentally changed what Bofurin represented. Rather than a gang focused on violence and territory, he transformed it into an organization dedicated to protecting the town and its residents. His vision turned former delinquents into guardians, earning the community's trust and gratitude.

The Rooftop Garden

Umemiya's beloved rooftop garden serves as both his sanctuary and a symbol of his philosophy. He personally tends to vegetables and plants, finding peace in watching things grow. The garden represents his belief that with proper care and attention, anything can flourish—including the young people under his guidance at Furin High.

Current Leadership

As Bofurin's leader, Umemiya maintains peace in the area while mentoring the next generation. He takes particular interest in helping new students like Sakura understand what Bofurin truly stands for. His leadership has created a legacy where strength is measured not by who you can defeat, but by who and what you can protect.
