Character Overview

Haruka Sakura is the main protagonist of Wind Breaker, a first-year student at Furin High School with a fierce reputation and distinctive appearance. Born with heterochromia—one yellow eye and one blue eye—and split-toned black and white hair, Sakura has always stood out from the crowd, often facing discrimination and isolation throughout his childhood.

Despite his rough exterior and tendency to get into fights, Sakura possesses a strong sense of justice and an unwavering desire to protect those who cannot protect themselves. His journey at Furin High marks a turning point in his life, where he learns the true meaning of strength, friendship, and community.

Personality & Traits

Combat Style

Sakura relies on raw power and instinct in battle. His fighting style is aggressive and direct, overwhelming opponents with powerful strikes and relentless determination. While initially lacking formal technique, his natural talent and combat experience make him a formidable fighter.

Social Skills

Despite his tough demeanor, Sakura is surprisingly shy and awkward in social situations. Years of isolation have left him inexperienced with positive attention and genuine kindness. He often doesn't know how to respond when people treat him well, leading to embarrassed reactions and red-faced denials.

Core Values

Justice and protection drive Sakura's actions. He has zero tolerance for those who prey on the weak and will immediately step in to defend others, regardless of personal risk. His strong moral compass guides his decisions, even when it puts him in difficult situations.

Background Story

Early Life

Sakura's childhood was marked by isolation and prejudice due to his unique appearance. His heterochromia and distinctive hair color made him a target for bullying and exclusion. Rather than succumbing to this treatment, Sakura learned to fight, using his fists to survive and protect himself from those who would harm him.

Arrival at Furin High

Seeking to prove himself as the strongest, Sakura deliberately chose to attend Furin High School, known for its reputation as a school of delinquents. However, he quickly discovered that Furin and its organization, Bofurin, were nothing like he expected. Instead of mindless violence, he found a group dedicated to protecting the town and its people.

Joining Bofurin

Initially resistant to the idea of working with others, Sakura gradually comes to understand the true purpose of Bofurin. Through encounters with upperclassmen like Hajime Umemiya and peers like Hayato Suo and Akihiko Nirei, he begins to see the value in fighting not just for himself, but for others and the community.

Current Role

As a first-year member of Bofurin, Sakura has become an integral part of the organization's efforts to protect the town. His raw power and determination, combined with his growing understanding of teamwork, make him a valuable asset. He continues to face challenges that test not only his fighting skills but also his emotional growth and ability to trust others.
